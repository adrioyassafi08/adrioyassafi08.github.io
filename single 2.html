I still remember the day I walked into the Bogor Chapter Selection, unsure of what to expect but determined to give my best. What followed was an experience that shaped my confidence, leadership, and ambition in ways I never imagined.

KL-YES (Kennedy-Lugar Youth Exchange and Study) is a full scholarship programme from the United States government for high school students (grades 10-11) to gain a one-year student exchange experience in the United States.

Participating in the Bogor Chapter Selection and advancing to the third stage among hundreds or even thousands of applicants has been a meaningful milestone in my journey. Although I did not progress to the national level, the experience gave me significant personal and academic growth. I had the opportunity to connect with students from diverse backgrounds across Bogor, learn to collaborate effectively, and build mutual trust in a competitive setting. More importantly, this experience became an early stepping stone toward my long-term goals. Throughout the process, I strengthened my public speaking, developed leadership qualities, and cultivated the confidence I need to pursue my aspirations.

Adrio Ahmad Yassafi

Gran Depok City, Cluster Anggrek 3 Depok, West Java, Indonesia
